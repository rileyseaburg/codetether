# CodeTether AI Video Ad - Facebook Campaign

## Brand / Product Name
CodeTether - AI Tasks That Actually Finish

## Product Description (Short)
AI automation that delivers real files. Trigger once, get CSV/PDF/code delivered by email or webhook. Works with Zapier, n8n, Make. Powered by MIT research.

## Product Description (Long)
CodeTether is the AI worker that finishes what ChatGPT can't. Run 5-60 minute tasks in the background and get real deliverables - CSV files, PDF reports, code, summaries - delivered automatically via email or webhook.

Powered by Recursive Language Models (RLM) from MIT research, CodeTether processes 10M+ tokens without degradation. While ChatGPT times out or loses context, CodeTether keeps working.

## Key Features (Bullet Points)
- Delivers real files: CSV, PDF, code, reports
- Runs 5-60 minutes unattended
- 10M+ token processing via RLM
- Works with Zapier, n8n, Make, webhooks
- Email delivery with attachments
- No babysitting required

## Target Audience
- Marketing managers doing lead scoring
- Agency owners creating client reports
- Consultants analyzing data
- Small business owners automating workflows
- Anyone tired of copy/pasting from ChatGPT

## Pain Points We Solve
1. **The Copy/Paste Problem** - ChatGPT gives text, you format it. We deliver ready-to-use files.
2. **The Babysitting Problem** - Complex tasks need constant steering. We run and finish automatically.
3. **The Timeout Problem** - Zapier AI steps fail after 30 seconds. We run up to 60 minutes.
4. **The Context Limit Problem** - Normal LLMs break on big data. We process 10M+ tokens.
